# ReviewSignal — buyer objectives ↔ product surface

Internal reference for GTM and UX alignment. The in-app copy follows this map.

| Buyer job | Question they need answered | Where in app | What “good” looks like |
|-----------|----------------------------|--------------|-------------------------|
| IR / leadership | Did store tone line up with moves? What not to claim? | **Overview → For leadership / IR**, **Market & stats** | One-sentence verdict, association ≠ causation, worst-day context |
| Product / support | What broke around bad days? What language? | **Overview → For product & support**, **Reviews**, **Themes** | Worst-day excerpts, disagreement queue, phrases/topics |
| Monitoring / ops | What to watch on the next export? | **Overview → For your next check-in**, **Key findings** | Same column mapping, Focus line, stable cadence |

Optional: compare consecutive exports (same mapping) to see **what changed** — not implemented in the PoC UI yet.
